Rules & Regulations

  • Maintain silence and respect inside the prayer halls at all times.
  • Ensure a modest dress code is observed while on the masjid premises.
  • Turn off or silence all mobile phones and electronic devices during prayers.
  • Keep the masjid premises clean and tidy, disposing of any waste properly.
